NOTICE OF APPRECIATION

Thank you to those who attended last year's 25th Guerrero Family Reunion for the first time. It was a huge success and we also thank those families who have attended the reunion year after year and have been helping with the information for the family tree. Please continue to send any corrections or additions to the family tree to Pancho Guerrero at frnqueq@sbcglobal.net.

Forgive us for not being able to spend more quality time chatting and catching up with all of you this past year. We also were not able to have all of the games and activities for children and adults for lack of more time.

We can begin to prepare and plan now for this years' reunion which will be held on Friday and Saturday, July 18 & 19, 2008. How can you help this year? Maybe we will need to begin planning activities on Friday, July 18th so that we can have time for all of the games and activities.

If you would like to keep in touch and get information about the family history, you may visit the website: www.familiaguerrerodelsurdezacatecas.com and send us pictures, especially of those family members who are no longer with us. Furthermore, we are planning to have on the website all family events and activities that you would like to post throughout the year. Such as Bachelor and Bachelorette parties, weddings, anniversaries, baby showers, birth announcements, baptisms, first communions, quinceañeras, birthdays, divorces, and funerals, etc.

We also need to continue to support the maintenance of the Guerrero Family website. Get involved by sending us pictures of new and old family members. Let us know if you would or would not like to post photos and names of your family. We look forward to hearing your suggestions or comments

 

Francisco Q. Guerrero
