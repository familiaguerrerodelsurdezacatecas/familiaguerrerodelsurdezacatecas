 To all Family members.

Just to keep every one updated: Aunt Rose had an appointment Monday June 24th with two radiation specialists. After a conference between the two specialist it was decided that the best option for Rose is the 35 session radiation treatment. Beginning in a couple of weeks she will be getting one session of radiation daily, Monday through Friday at the Norris Center next to General Hospital.

Of course Rose is quite disappointed because after surgery she was told she might need just one session of radiation.
She will have some loss of hair and other mild side effects.

Her close friends and family are trying to keep her attitude high with visits, prayers and well wishes.



Thank you for keeping her in your thoughts and prayers.



Gloria Goodman
